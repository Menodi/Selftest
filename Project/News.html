Campus Initiative Launch

Date: September 10, 2025

Everbright is going green! We’re launching a new sustainability program that includes on-campus composting, energy-efficient upgrades, and a tree-planting event in collaboration with the Everbright Environmental Club. The university aims to reduce its carbon footprint by 40% by 2030.

🎨 Art & Design Showcase: “Perspectives”

Date: November 12–14, 2025

Presented by the School of Art & Design, this exhibition highlights student projects from Interior Design, Fine Arts, and Architecture. Come explore thought-provoking installations, 3D models, and digital art celebrating the theme of “Perspectives”.
