Introduction to characters from anime



Yae Miko is elegant and composed, with a mischievous streak. She enjoys teasing others, especially her close friends like Raiden Shogun (Ei). Despite her playful nature, she is highly intelligent and deeply spiritual, overseeing religious rituals and the shrine. She’s also the owner of a successful publishing company, often indulging in light novels. Yae Miko







Akame is quiet, serious, and highly disciplined. Though she often appears emotionless, she deeply cares for her comrades and feels intense guilt over the lives she has taken. Her upbringing as a child soldier in the Empire's assassination unit hardened her, but she retains a strong sense of justice.


Kirito is a highly skilled gamer and swordsman who becomes trapped in the virtual reality MMORPG Sword Art Online, where dying in the game means dying in real life. Known as the “Black Swordsman” for his dark outfit and solo fighting style, Kirito quickly rises as one of the top players thanks to his sharp instincts and technical prowess.
